Body Description (anatomy)

Slimes are typically unisex and capable of reproducing asexually.  NekoGoo's body can take any shape but often defaults to feminine because soft & bouncy is an easier form to maintain.  NekoGoo is quite indifferent to these matters and willing to change shape and have as few (or many) body parts as pleases their partner.

Their body emits a faint light (akin to a glowing mushroom) and slime surface is usually shimmering, appearing reminiscent of someone glistening with sweat. 

tldr: androgynous head, feminine body, can have all or none of the things

Cosmic Cat Eyes

Each of NekoGoo's "eyes" appear as a vast purple-starred cosmos with a large spiralling "pupil" of mana/energy emanating from the inner [invisible] slime core.  The cat-like vertical slit at its center is a warped black hole.
