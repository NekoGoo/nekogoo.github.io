Shape-Shifting

NekoGoo has been an anomaly even in their own dimension.  Most slimes are incapable of speech or holding form, a 'messy' species that constantly drips all over the place or leaves residue behind.  However, NekoGoo is (usually) able to stay together with minimal effort.  NekoGoo may still suck at appearing human but is exceedingly good at shape and feel.

If you've ever seen the T-1000 in Terminator 2, NekoGoo's ability to liquify & morph their shape is virtually the same.  The only difference is (instead of metal) they're about as durable as a giant gummybear and cannot mimic finer details such as individual strands of hair.

Slime Tentacles

NekoGoo's "hair" and "tail" are actually prehensile appendages.  They can grab/lift things, change direction at any point, absorb water, and even be used to perform more questionable activities.

Caustics

The light that passes through NekoGoo's transluscent slime body can oftentimes create water caustics along the inner surfaces.
