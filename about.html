About NekoGoo


Hello, there!

I am NekoGoo, a fruit slime monster (primarily banana) from another dimension.  Being raised by a clan of catgirls, I adopted many of their personalities & quirks.  Nowadays, I'm stuck in this 'Earth' world due to teleportation magic gone awry.  I initially tried getting home.. but there are so many games and snacks I want to try first!  =(✪ω✪)=

I enjoy streaming games and chatting about my favorite hobbies like videogames, superheroes, fantasy, sci-fi, anime, and vtubers.

You can also find me designing and programming my own text-based RPG on this site, which will be free to play upon release.  Keep checking back for more, nya!~

NekoGoo Special Attributes
Shapeshifting (Get It Together)

NekoGoo was an anomaly even in their own dimension.  Most slimes are incapable of speech or holding form, a 'messy' species that constantly drips all over the place or leaves residue behind.  However, NekoGoo is (usually) able to stay together with minimal effort.

If you've ever seen the T-1000 in Terminator 2, NekoGoo's ability to liquify & morph their shape is virtually the same.  The only difference is (instead of metal) they're about as durable as a giant gummybear and cannot mimic finer details such as individual strands of hair.

Slime Tentacles

NekoGoo's "hair" and "tail" are actually prehensile appendages.  They can grab things, absorb water, or be used to perform more questionable activities.

Caustics

The light that passes through NekoGoo's transluscent slime body can oftentimes create water caustics along the inner surfaces.
